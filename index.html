How to Control via Mobile

  1. Ensure your Phone and Laptop are connected to the SAME WiFi network.
  2. Open your phone's Camera and Scan the QR Code shown in the app header.
  3. Alternatively, open your phone's browser and type the URL shown (e.g. http://192.168.x.x:3030).
Not connecting? Ensure Windows Firewall is allowing the app to communicate on Private networks.
